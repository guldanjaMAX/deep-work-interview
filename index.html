Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

100% free. The Deep Work Interview is my way of helping coaches and consultants get crystal clear on their positioning and pricing. No upsell, no strings attached.

How long does the interview take?

The live session is about 2 hours. You'll get the detailed report within 3-5 business days. Total time investment from you is minimal.

What if I'm not ready for this?

You don't need to be perfect or have all the answers. We figure it out together. The interview is designed to help you get clarity even if you're still working things out.

What's included in the report?

Named competitors with their pricing, market positioning analysis, your credibility score (1-10), three specific service offers with pricing, full website blueprint with copy, and email templates.

Can I use this for my team?

This is designed for individual coaches and consultants, but we can discuss group interviews. Just mention it when you schedule.

Do you work with all industries?

Primarily coaches and consultants in business strategy, executive coaching, sales, marketing, and career coaching. If you're unsure if your niche fits, schedule a call and we'll figure it out.
