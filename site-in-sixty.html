  • 1
    The Claude Interview Process
    ▾

    This is where the magic happens. Instead of filling out forms or sitting on discovery calls with an agency, you're going to have a collaborative conversation with Claude that extracts your vision and turns it into a real website.

    Your role in this process is to be the editor, not the creator. Claude will generate ideas, options, and directions. Your job is to pick what resonates, discard what does not, and refine until it feels right.

    Module 1 Walkthrough (9 min) Full Build Session (Bonus)

    Step 1: Start the Conversation

    Open Claude at claude.ai and start a new conversation. Your opening message should dump everything you have and invite Claude to interview you for what's missing.

    I want to build a website for [YOUR IDEA/BUSINESS/EVENT].

Here's everything I have so far:
[Paste any existing copy, descriptions, taglines, or your DWI blueprint]

Here's what I want people to do when they visit:
[Book a call? Buy something? Sign up for an event?]

Here are some websites I like the style of:
[Paste URLs if you have them]

Interview me to figure out what else you need to build this site.

    Don't have brand colors yet?

    No problem. Use this prompt to define your visual identity first:

    I need help defining the visual identity for my website before we build it.

My business: [DESCRIBE WHAT YOU DO IN ONE SENTENCE]
The feeling I want visitors to have: [PICK 3 WORDS]
Websites I admire the look of: [PASTE 2 TO 3 URLs IF YOU HAVE THEM]

Please suggest: a color palette (5 colors with hex codes), font pairings for headings and body text, and an overall visual direction. Explain your choices so I can give feedback.

    Step 2: Upload Your Assets

    Testimonials (screenshots or text files), photos (up to 20 at a time), and screenshots of websites you like.

    Step 3: Answer the Interview Questions

    Claude will ask you questions. Answer honestly and thoroughly. At some point, Claude will tell you how confident it is that it understands your vision. That's your signal you're close to ready.

    Step 4: Focus on Above the Fold First

    The hero section (what people see before scrolling) is 90% of your website's impact. Tell Claude: "Let's focus on the hero section first. I want to nail the headline, subheadline, and main call to action before we build the rest."

  • 2
    Generating the Website Files
    ▾

    Once you're happy with the concept, it's time to generate the actual website files.

    File Generation Walkthrough

    The Magic Prompt

    Copy this exact prompt to get deployment-ready files:

    Please generate a complete, deployment-ready website as a zip file.

Requirements:
Single index.html file with embedded CSS (no separate stylesheet)
Fully responsive design (mobile-first)
Clean, modern styling that matches the visual direction we discussed
Include all the sections we discussed
For images, embed as base64 or include in the zip
Ready to upload directly to Cloudflare Pages with no modifications
Smooth scroll behavior between sections
Subtle hover effects on buttons and links
Make sure the design does NOT look like a generic AI template. Use the specific colors, fonts, and visual choices we agreed on. Add small design details that make it feel handcrafted.

    Claude will generate a zip file containing your index.html and any images. This is your first live draft. It won't be perfect, and that's the point. Get it live, then refine.

  • 3
    Deploying to Cloudflare Pages
    ▾

    This is the part people think is technical. It's not. It's literally drag and drop.

    Deployment Walkthrough

    The 7 Step Deploy

    1. Log into Cloudflare at cloudflare.com

    2. Navigate to Compute & AI, then Workers & Pages in the left sidebar

    3. Click Create, then select "Pages" and "Upload assets"

    4. Name your project (this becomes your temporary URL)

    5. Upload your files by dragging your zip into the upload area

    6. Click Deploy and wait 30 to 60 seconds

    7. View your live site at yourproject.pages.dev

    That's it. Your website is now live on the internet.

  • 4
    Connecting Your Custom Domain
    ▾

    Your site is live on .pages.dev. Now let's connect your real domain.

    Domain Connection Walkthrough

    If You Bought Through Cloudflare

    In your Pages project, click "Custom domains," then "Set up a domain," enter your domain, and Cloudflare automatically configures everything. Wait 5 to 15 minutes for the SSL certificate.

    If Your Domain Is Elsewhere

    Add your domain to Cloudflare. Cloudflare gives you two nameservers. Update nameservers at your registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.). Wait for propagation (usually under an hour). Then connect to Pages.

    Setting Up WWW and Non-WWW

    Add both as custom domains in Pages, then go to Rules, then Redirect Rules and use the pre-built "Redirect from WWW to Root" snippet.

  • 5
    Making Changes After Launch
    ▾

    Your site is live but you want to make changes. Here's the simple workflow.

    Update Workflow

    Go back to Claude. Describe what you want to change. Ask for an updated zip. In Cloudflare Pages, click "Create new deployment" (not "Create application"). Upload and deploy. Changes go live within seconds.

    Cloudflare keeps every previous deployment, so if something breaks you can roll back instantly. You will never lose your working site by trying something new.

  • 6
    Level Up Your Site
    ▾

    Your site is live. Now let's make it great. Use whichever prompt matches what you're feeling right now.

    Quality Control Walkthrough

    "My site looks like every other AI site"

    Look at my current website [PASTE URL or upload screenshot].
I want it to feel [PICK 3 WORDS] but right now it feels generic.
Please audit the design and suggest specific changes: colors, contrast, fonts, white space, and small design details that could make this feel more polished. For each suggestion, give me the specific CSS change. Then generate an updated zip with all your recommendations applied.

    "People visit but nobody clicks anything"

    My website is live at [YOUR URL or upload screenshots].
The main action I want visitors to take is: [BOOK A CALL / BUY / SIGN UP]
Right now, not enough people are doing that.
Please audit this page for conversion issues: headline clarity, CTA visibility, trust signals near decision points, confusing elements, and mobile tap distance. Give me your top 5 changes ranked by impact. Then generate an updated zip.

    "I need better images"

    Use Nano Banana (nanobanana.io), Google's free AI image generator. No account needed. Describe what you want in plain language and it creates it.

    "I need to show up on Google"

    I need to optimize my website for search engines. My primary goal is to rank for [WHAT SOMEONE WOULD SEARCH TO FIND YOU].
Please add: compelling meta title and description, Open Graph tags, schema markup for [YOUR BUSINESS TYPE], proper heading structure, alt text for every image, and clean URL structure. Also review my content for search keywords. Generate an updated zip with all SEO improvements applied.

    "I need a contact form"

    I need to add a contact form that sends submissions to my email.
Please add a clean form section using Formspree (formspree.io) as the backend.
Include fields for: [NAME, EMAIL, MESSAGE]
Add validation, a success message, and make sure it looks good on mobile.
Use the action URL: https://formspree.io/f/[I WILL REPLACE THIS]
Generate an updated zip with the form integrated.

    "I want to sell something on my site"

    I want to add a way for visitors to pay me directly from my website.
What I am selling: [DESCRIBE PRODUCT/SERVICE AND PRICE]
Please add a clear Buy Now button, style it to match my design, add supporting text near the button, and make sure it works on mobile.
I will use a Stripe payment link: [PASTE STRIPE LINK HERE]
Generate an updated zip with the payment button integrated.
